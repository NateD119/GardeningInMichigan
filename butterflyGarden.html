Butterfly Gardens

Butterfly Gardens are a integrel part of a healthy ecosysytem. When you hear the term "Butterfly Garden" one may think that you are just trying to attract the winged creatures for your own enjoyment, but really there are numerous benefits to planting one. And these gardens are only for butterflies, the state of Michigan has over 450 species of pillinating bees! Butterflies, bees, and other pollinators look for "targets". What that means, is that the flower in circular in shape, usually with petals around it, and a dark center. Something like a rosebush or Peony, with their many many layers of petals would not fit my "target" definition. Another tip would be to use color throughout the garden. Oranges, yellows, reds. The brighter the better.
